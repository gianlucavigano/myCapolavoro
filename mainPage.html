Welcome to QuicklyQPC

QuicklyQPC is a site designed primarily for developers who, during the development phase of websites or applications, need to find QRcodes, passwords or hexadecimal/rgba's codes quickly and easily. In fact, it is often common to have these needs in order to do quick and temporary tests within the code, and these 3 elements are the most requested and used. The strong point of this site is the fact that these 3 functionalities are contained and provided within it, avoiding the developer having to open multiple tabs in his browser and creating less confusion and clutter.
